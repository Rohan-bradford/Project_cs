About Me

Hello! I’m Rohan, a Computer Science student at the University of Bradford and a passionate ethical hacker. I’m ranked among the top 100 researchers on HackerOne and work as a freelance penetration tester.

I enjoy finding security vulnerabilities in web, mobile, and blockchain applications. My main strengths are recon automation, logic flaw discovery, and analyzing APIs and JavaScript for hidden bugs.

I’ve worked on real-world security projects, written custom tools, and helped secure platforms used by thousands of users. Outside of hacking, I enjoy scripting, exploring new technologies, and helping others in the cybersecurity community.
